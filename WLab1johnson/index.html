History

Konohagakure (木ノ葉隠れの里, Konohagakure no Sato also known as "Village Hidden in the Leaves"

During the Warring States Period, ninja were organised into small mercenary clans that knew nothing but battle. The two strongest of these clans were the Senju and the Uchiha, who had warred against each other for their entire histories. Eventually, intending to end the constant bloodshed, Hashirama Senju used his position as leader of his clan to broker a truce with the Uchiha clan through their leader, his childhood friend Madara Uchiha.

Location

Things to do in Konoha

Konoha, also known as the Hidden Leaf Village, is a central location in the Naruto universe and the hometown of Naruto and other characters. It's known for its rich ninja history and as a training ground for aspiring ninjas

The final battle

The final battle between Naruto and Sasuke is considered one of the most iconic anime fights. In the battle, Sasuke absorbs Tailed Beast chakra in an attempt to kill Naruto. Naruto tells Sasuke that he won't let Sasuke land any punches like he did last time, and that he won't give Sasuke the title of Hokage. Naruto says that he desires revolution and that the Hokage he speaks of is different from Itachi Uchiha, his brother who protected the village from the shadows
